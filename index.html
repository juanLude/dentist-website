Oral Hygiene & Gum Disease

Good oral hygiene isn't only important for a healthy smile. It can also impact on your overall health and wellbeing. Daily oral care at home and regular check-ups and professional teeth cleaning with our dental hygienist improve your chances of living free from cavities, bad breath and painful dental problems.


White Fillings

One of the most common dental procedures, white fillings are suitable for patients of all ages, including children. We use white composite fillings to fill in cavities and small cracks in teeth, or to restore a chipped or worn-down tooth. Unlike old metal amalgam fillings that are very obvious in the mouth, white fillings are made from a dental resin composite that our dentists can match to your original tooth shade. This offers a more discreet and natural-looking finish.


Orthodontics

Orthodontic treatment can help to straighten crooked or misshapen teeth, close gaps and correct bite problems.
